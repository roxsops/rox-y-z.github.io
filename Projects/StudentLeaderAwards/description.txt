Student Leader Awards
---
Campaign design for The Student Leader Awards of 2018 at California State University, Los Angeles. This annual ceremony recognizes and celebrates the many activities, events and contributions of on-campus student organizations, members and advisers. To reflect the focus on leadership for this ceremony, the golden eagle side-profile design was anchored to one side.
