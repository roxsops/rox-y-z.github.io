Student Leader Awards
---
Campaign design for The Student Leader Awards of 2018 at California State University, Los Angeles. This annual ceremony recognizes and celebrates the many activities, events and contributions of on-campus student organizations, members and advisers. To reflect that, a golden eagle design was positioned on one side to signify leadership.
