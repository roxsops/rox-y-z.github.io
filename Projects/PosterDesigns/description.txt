Poster Designs
---
Various Poster Designs from events hosted by the University-Student Union at California State University, Los Angeles. Events were in collaboration with a dedicated event coordinator on campus, each of whom were eager to bring students both entertaining and meaningful things to do on campus. A big focus when designing was to be captivating, relevant, and inclusive.
